Main Locations

The Blighted Village

A hidden cellar beneath the Hag's hut contains a portal to the Underdark, guarded by a territorial Bulette. Be prepared for a fight if you want to explore the depths!

The Goblin Camp

Within the camp lies a hidden stash of cursed items, including the infamous "Boots of Dancing" that will make you jig uncontrollably.

The Druid Grove

Deep within the heart of the Grove, whispers speak of a hidden grove tended by a mischievous Sprite who grants wishes, but at a potentially hefty price.

The Ancient Crypt

Lurking within the shadows of the crypt's mausoleum is a spectral Lich seeking a worthy successor. Do you dare offer yourself?
